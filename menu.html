How to Play

Objective: Destroy notes by identifying the correct musical pitches!

  • Notes will move from right to left toward the clef
  • Type the correct note letter (A, B, C, D, E, F, G) or click the pitch buttons
  • If a note reaches the clef, you lose a life
  • Wrong answers cause an explosion on the clef and you lose a life
  • Correct answers create a laser from your ship to destroy the note
  • Every 10 correct answers advances you to the next level with faster notes
  • You start with 3 lives and get bonus lives at levels 4 and 8
  • Survive as long as possible and get the highest score!
